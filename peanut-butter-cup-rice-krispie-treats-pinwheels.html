    It’s been way too long since I shared a Reese’s peanut butter cup recipe. If you’ve been following along here at MOTO for any length of time you are probably pretty aware of my deep and abiding love for all things Reese’s. Hence, my firstborn – Reece.

    Today I’ve got a play off of the most popular recipe on my site – S’mores Rice Krispies Treats Pinwheels. I changed up a few things and ended up with a treat that even my super self-controlled hubby couldn’t resist: Peanut Butter Cup Rice Krispie Treats Pinwheels!

    These are really super simple to make. The base of the treats in cocoa krispies, butter, and marshmallows – just like a regular rice krispie treat. It gets pressed into a jelly roll pan that has been lined with parchment paper and generously buttered.

    After pressing down the krispie treats, I mixed peanut butter and marshmallow fluff together to form a sticky layer that gets spread of the rice krispie treats with an offset spatula. Finally, I chopped up a bunch of Reese’s peanut butter cups and spread them over the top. Pressing the peanut butter cups lightly into the peanut butter-marshmallow fluff layer ensures they won’t fall off when you roll it up. Roll it all up – just like a cinnamon roll and place in the fridge to chill.

    It doesn’t take long, maybe an hour or so, for everything to set up. Slice and enjoy!

    These pinwheels are awesome for parties because the recipe makes a bunch.

    Peanut Butter Cup Rice Krispie Treats Pinwheels

    These Peanut Butter Cup Rice Krispie Treats Pinwheels are my new favorite treat and sure to be a hit with the peanut butter lover in your life. Layers of chocolate, marshmallow, peanut butter and peanut butter cups are rolled up into an irresistible dessert! Great for parties!

    Course Dessert Cuisine American Keyword peanut butter cup rice krispie treats pinwheels Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Total Time 5 minutes minutes Servings 24 servings Calories 158kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    Instructions

    • Line a 10x15 jelly roll pan with parchment paper. Butter the paper and set aside.

    • Combine marshmallows and one quarter cup of butter in a microwave-safe bowl. Cover and heat on high for 30 seconds, stir. Repeat until completely blended.

    • Stir in cocoa rice krispies.

    • Press mixture into prepared pan.

    • Place marshmallow creme and peanut butter into a microwave safe bowl and microwave for 30 seconds or until easily stirred. Spread marshmallow-peanut butter mixture on top or rice krispies layer using an offset spatula.

    • Sprinkle chopped peanut butter cups on top and press down lightly.

    • Roll up jelly roll style (like a cinnamon roll), starting on the long side. Peel away the paper as you roll. Pinch the seams together.

    • Place seam side down and refrigerate for 30 to 45 minutes or until chocolate has set.

    • Slice and serve!
