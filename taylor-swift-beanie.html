Swift sported a Kansas City Chiefs beanie that the internet fawned over at last Sunday’s game, paired with an oversized gray apparel sweatshirt, plaid pleated skirt, and matching red lip.

    Shown in the fan’s TikTok video, the original beanie is handmade and features the football team’s colors: white and red, with the number 87 sewn in on the band and a multicolored pom pom to top it off. Taylor has been repping vintage Chiefs merch that has been selling out left and right since she was first spotted supporting her boyfriend, NFL tight end Travis Kelce. Remember that denim jacket Kelce showed off as a nod to Swift? What about that classic black turtleneck Swift wore in the audience? Yeah, us and our credit cards do, too. The fan crocheted the viral beanie the night before the game with the intention of giving it to Taylor but was totally shocked when it actually appeared on the music star.
