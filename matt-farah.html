    Matt Farah (born December 1, 1981) is an American YouTube Content Creator, Filmmaker, Host, Car Enthusiast, and Social Media Personality from Venice Beach, Los Angeles, California.

    He is famous for being one of the most followed internet personalities. Matt has a YouTube channel “TheSmokingTire”. Furthermore, the YouTuber currently has more than 925+ k subscribers on the channel.

    Farah mainly entertains his fans by uploading videos like a podcast with other YouTubers & famous personalities, detail knowledge about various cars, watches reviews, Vlogs, and much more.

    Matt drives modified cars owned by regular people. These people created the custom-built rides which meet their specific performance goals. As a host, he works for channels like “/TUNED” and “/DRIVE”.

    Rise to Fame and Other Work Experience

    During an interview with the “Gears and Gasolina” channel, Matt told the viewers that he became attracted to cars at the age of 3. In fact, he was also able to tell the car name just by looking at their tires.

    As he grew up, Matt always wanted to pursue his career in the car industry but didn’t know what to do. Later, the content creator began working in various dealerships. Moreover, he also worked at “Gotham Dream Cars” (an exotic car rental company) for a while.

    There, Farah learned a lot about numerous exotic cars and developed his knowledge about them. In the starting days of his profession, the YouTuber opened a car wash company along with his friend.

    This led to the starting of a driving club where many people went on long drives. One day, Matt’s friend Tom brought a camera and asked him to host a car show. After one week, someone told him and said that he runs a YouTube channel and offered him to work with him.

    YouTube Career and Net worth

    In addition, the car enthusiast launched his YouTube channel on June 23, 2009. His video was “Ford SVT Raptor: Built for Baja, Tested on Bullrun” which came out on August 03, 2009.

    Currently, Matt also stars in “The Car Show” on Speed YouTube channel with Adam Carolla, John Sally, and Dan Neil. The YouTuber mainly earns his money from sponsorships, selling merch on various social media, and advertisements on his website and YouTube channel.
