    Paris Hilton is a lot of things. She’s an author. She’s a DJ. She’s a water bottle designer. She’s the owner of the trademarked phrase, “That’s hot.” And now, she’s coming for you, Martha, with a new line of (pink and gold, natch) kitchenware. The Be An Icon line is available at Walmart, so we asked Paris to give us her top 11 items (if you’re a Paris fan, you know why she chose 11 items) to buy from Walmart—including groceries and her top Be An Icon picks. Slip into your matching pink sweatsuit, get that hair in a high pony, and bookmark this list for the next time you hit the Walmart aisles. Here’s Paris Hilton’s top 11 picks for things you should be buying at Walmart.

    Be An Icon Popcorn Popper

    “I am totally obsessed with this popcorn popper—it’s the ultimate accessory for sliving snacks at home,” says Paris. (If you don’t speak fluent Paris, “sliving” is a portmanteau of slaying and living.) “It’s easy to use, dishwasher safe, and the dual-function lid for measuring kernels and melting butter is just *chef’s kiss*.”

    Orville Redenbacher’s Original Popcorn

    When it comes to her popcorn pick, Paris sticks to a classic. The bespectacled Mr. Redenbacher makes a darn fine popping corn, whether you like it plain with salt, drowning in butter, or tossed with every spice in your cabinet.

    Orville Redenbacher’s Movie Theater Butter Seasoning

    Paris goes full-on movie theater with her popcorn—without dirtying another pot or bowl. With this seasoning you can sprinkle instant movie theater butter flavor onto your popcorn, no melted butter needed (but always appreciated, if you’re having us over).

    Be An Icon Tea Kettle

    “Whether I’m spilling tea or sipping it, I swear by this iconic and chic tea kettle,” says Paris. “Its pink heart design makes it a true star in my kitchen.” The handle stays cool so you won’t burn your hand. So, even when it’s…hot…you’ll stay cool.

    Celestial Seasonings Herbal Tea Sampler

    That sleepy bear is a style icon, just like Paris. So, it’s no wonder she picks him and his tea, along with a selection of other soothing blends, to stock her tea drawer.

    Be An Icon Mini Fridge

    “My mini refrigerator is my beauty secret weapon,” Paris says. “Not only does it add a touch of glamor to my vanity with its mirrored door and dimmable LED light, but it’s also a game-changer for my skincare routine. I love keeping all my face masks, serums, and creams at the perfect cool temperature and creating a spa-like experience for myself every day.” Of course, it’s equally good at keeping other things cold—like a selection of pink drinks (we’ve got a favorite canned sparkling rosé in mind).

    Be An Icon Heart Cake Tin

    Yes, Paris bakes. “Baking is like therapy for me, and my heart cake tin is the secret ingredient to making it extra special. The heart-shaped design adds a touch of cuteness to every dessert,” says Paris. Bonus: It’s dishwasher safe.

    Pillsbury Funfetti Cake Mix

    Celebrities, they’re just like us! Paris understands that Funfetti cake is simply the best cake you can make at home.

    Betty Crocker Rich & Creamy Vanilla Frosting

    Vanilla is not plain. It’s a delicious flavor—especially when it’s in the form of frosting. And it’s the only way to top a Funfetti cake.

    Great Value Sprinkles

    Did you think Paris was done with her heart-shaped Funfetti cake? She is not. Add sprinkles! Walmart’s Great Value sprinkles (rainbow, of course) are her pick.

    Cake Decorating Kit

    Paris is not known for her restraint. If she’s going to do something, she’s going to do it. Her final pick for her Walmart haul is a 68-piece cake decorating set so she can create an over-the-top cake that is nothing short of iconic.
