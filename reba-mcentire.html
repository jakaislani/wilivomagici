    If you love country music, then you probably know about the "Queen of Country," Reba McEntire. She is an American singer, songwriter, actress, and record producer. McEntire has sold more than 75 million records worldwide, being one of the best-selling artists of all time.

    Reba is the recipient of numerous prestigious awards, including Country Music Association, Kennedy Center Honor, etc. among others. Her 28 albums have been certified gold, platinum or multi-platinum in sales by the Recording Industry Association of America. Many of her songs have been listed in the top numbers of the various playlists.

    Is Reba McEntire dating anyone

    The 64-year-old singer is currently single. In the past, she has been highlighted in many relationships, but she is enjoying life as a single mom. Previously, she dated Anthony Skeeter Lasuzzo with whom she met during a vacation in Jackson Hole, Wyoming, in 2017. 

    The pair officially confirmed to the world about their relationship after making a walk-in red carpet at the 2018 Grammys. But within two years of their relationship, they called it quit to their relationship in 2019. 

    First husband, Charlie Battles

    McEntire and Charlie's first encounter took place back at the end of 1974. Both were very attracted to each other and started dating. After dating more than a year, the pair tied the knot in 1976; at that time, Reba was only twenty-one-years-old while Battles was ten years senior than her. 

    The duo then shared a ranch in Oklahoma. After enjoying marital life over a decade, both call it to quit their relationship in a mutual understanding. Then-after, Reba moved to Nashville, Tennessee, to further pursue her career. 

    Shares one son with Narvel Blackstock

    The historic singer and her manager way crossed during Reba's concert in the early '80s at that Narvel Blackstock was still married to his first wife. 

    Initially, he became the manager of her and helped her to gain more fame and name. So, their closeness grew stronger, and later, they exchanged marital vows on 3rd June 1989 after dating for over eight years. 

    Their marital life leads them to share one son named Shelby Blackstock, who was born on 23rd February 1990. As of now, he is one of the successful American race car drivers who is married to singer Kelly Clarkson.

    End of relationship with mutual understanding
