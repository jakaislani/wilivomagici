    The name H.E.R., which stands for “Having Everything Revealed,” was given to Gabriella Sarmiento Wilson on June 27, 1997.

    She is an American singer, composer, and musician. Along with nominations for a Golden Globe Award, three American Music Awards, and four Billboard Music Awards, she has won various accolades, including an Academy Award, a Children’s and Family Emmy Award, and five Grammy Awards.

    Whats does H.E.R. mean?

    As mentioned early on the name actually means “Having Everything Revealed,”

    After making her first appearances in music and releasing songs under her true name, H.E.R. made a comeback in 2016 and released her first EP H.E.R. Volume 1 via RCA, which was followed by four further EPs.

    Her debut collection of songs, H.E.R. (2017), which included six more songs in addition to the songs from her first two EPs, was nominated for five Grammy Awards and won Best R&B Album and Best R&B Performance.
